Emerald Is My Cat


I have a Cat,
She is not fat,
Because she doesn't eat kit-kats often.

She chases rats,
She plays on her mat,
And she loves pet from mommy.

Emerald is her name,
She is not to blame,
She was called that because she's pretty.

She has no kittys,
Which is a pitty,
And, She is usually not giddy!
